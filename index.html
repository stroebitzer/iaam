Infrastructure as a Meetup

We are Testers, SysAdmins, DBAs, Ops, DevOps, Manager, Devs,... who meet every 3 months to discuss DevOps things. Besides very technical stuff we adore to talk about growing a culture which lets the DevOps idea grow.

We love to talk about things like Container, Docker, Ansible, Salt, Linux, Continuous Deployment, Continuous Delivery,... in a very interactive way.
